About

We use mushrooms and mycelium as both subject and lens—curiosity first, jargon last. Each conversation branches from real practice: cultivation that actually works, foraging that respects habitats, and lab ideas that escape the lab.

Expect growers with dirt under their nails, researchers with data in hand, and makers testing the edge—from nutrition and cognition to materials and remediation. If it connects to the network, it’s fair game.

Grow & Forage

Practical home-grow (substrates, spawn, fruiting) and fieldcraft—responsible ID in the wild.

Mind & Body

Cognition, mood, immunity, performance—what’s real, what’s hype, and safe practice.

R&D & Tech

Mycelium materials, textiles, bioprocessing, space research, and the weird/cool edge of fungi.

Policy & Ethics

Psilocybin reform, regulation, access, and the ethics shaping mycology’s future.
